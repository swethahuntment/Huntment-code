About Cafe Staywoke (Mood Shine Food)

Cafe Staywoke is inspired by the millennial concept of being woke, which defines the idea of being aware. The cafe serves global comfort food which brings food & beverage from around the world in a casual, approachable setting. Travel the world with your taste buds to seek an extraordinary and soulful experience.

Cafe Staywoke is part of Moonshine Food ventures Pvt. Ltd. (MFVL). Company aims to provide an unparalleled experience to all patrons by curating bespoke offerings.

Staywoke aims to curate a space that incites great, meaningful and like-minded conversations over comfort food& beverage. Staywoke uses locally produced ingredients, grown and made by real farmers and artisans ensuring fresh, delicious, gourmet woke meals for their guests. The café promotes carbon neutrality. 0.

One of the most trending cafes of Delhi-NCR, averaging 10K pax per month. EBITDA positive from month 1. Given revenue figures are easily achievable based on our existing store track records.

- Have no loans and a single individual owns the property.
